Tyler Yzaguirre

Tyler is an up and coming national voice for the Second Amendment. During his enrollment at the University of Delaware, Tyler started a registered student organization known as Students for the Second Amendment. This student organization grew to over 150 students during his Presidency, with the help of his Executive Board. Also during his time with Students for the Second Amendment, the Executive Board contracted Katie Pavlich from Fox News to speak at the University and school policy was changed to allow students to buy ammunition with University funds.
